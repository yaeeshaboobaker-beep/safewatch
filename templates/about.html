SafeWatch Initiative

Empowering Communities, Ensuring Safety.

About the Project

SafeWatch is an advanced systems architecture project developed to bridge the communication gap between local residents, neighborhood watches, and emergency responders in the eThekwini Municipality. By utilizing real-time geospatial reporting, skill-based dispatching, and automated severity triage, the system ensures that critical emergencies are addressed swiftly and efficiently.
